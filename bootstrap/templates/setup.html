Right now, you can only use Memu when you're home. To access it from anywhere (like at work or traveling), we need to create a secure tunnel to the internet.

The Simple Answer: Cloudflare creates a secure path to your home without making your address public or opening dangerous "ports" on your router.

The Problem: Cloudflare doesn't allow video streaming through their tunnel (it's in their rules). Since your photos and videos go through this tunnel, we're breaking their rules.

What We're Doing: We're switching to a better system (Headscale/Tailscale) that's designed for exactly this use case. But it's not ready yet.

What Should You Do? Skip this for now. You can set it up later when the new system is ready.
